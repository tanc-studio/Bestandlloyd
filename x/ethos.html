Ethos

We appreciate that our lights are often placed with exceptional furnishing and art and as such have to fit in seamlessly. We collaborate with artists who are technical perfectionists and unique in their fields, whether it be glassblowing, ceramics, wood or leather. The results are works of art. Some of the glass pieces weigh up to 20kilos before cutting and there are perhaps only 2 teams in the world capable of working at that weight. The leather is stitched by hand rather than machine to avoid the tell tale punch marks, instead each hole is made using a punch. Only thick blemish free vegetable dyed leather is used. The colour runs all the way through so the edges can be burnished by hand. Everything can be customised from glass colours and electrical cables down to the colour of the stitching thread. The result are pieces that exemplify craft and dying skills and are works of art in their own right.
