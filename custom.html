From the best Cut Crystal to the shiniest Polished Nickel we create timeless design

Best & Lloyd prides itself on being able to make anything in any finish and using any material. We use the services of 7 glass artists who specialise in different blowing styles and techniques, we can design, produce & engineer any type of metal work in our Birmingham factory and use both lost wax and sand casters for more intricate pieces. Our leather workers are extremely adept at working with any leather or shape and the same goes for carpentry, stonework & ceramics. Every piece in our current catalogue is a sculpture with a lampshade, made by artists who normally sell sculpture through galleries and as a result we carry very little stock and every piece is a one off commission. It is extremely easy for us to give our clients the widest opportunity to engage with that process and design something entirely new or adapt one of our existing designs if they so wish. Every lamp can be modified for use on Yachts.
